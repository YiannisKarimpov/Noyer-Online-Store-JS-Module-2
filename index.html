    LumiSquad - Light Heroes

    LumiSquad is a team of light-aligned heroes who fight to protect balance, life, and hope in a fractured world. Their strength lies not only in power, but in trust, discipline, and restraint. Each member carries light differently — as healing, judgment, chaos, or protection — but together they represent the belief that light does not need to dominate to endure.

    Dynamic

    Theme: Hope, balance, unity
    Style: Heroic, refined, resolute
    Role: Defenders of the world

    GrimLight - Dark Heroes

    Duskbound operates in the gray space between right and wrong. They confront threats that LumiSquad cannot, using forbidden methods, harsh decisions, and personal sacrifice. Where LumiSquad protects the light, Duskbound prevents the darkness from spreading — even if it costs them their reputation, morality, or lives.

    Theme: Sacrifice, secrecy, consequence
    Style: Gritty, tactical, relentless
    Role: Necessary shadows

    The Void Choir - Void Entities

    The Void Choir is not a traditional enemy, but an existential force that seeks to erase meaning, identity, and emotion from reality. Its members are echoes of lost selves, bound together by a shared doctrine of unbeing. They do not conquer — they silence, rewrite, and unmake.

    Theme: Oblivion, corruption, loss of self
    Style: Eldritch, ritualistic, unsettling
    Role: Reality-level threat
